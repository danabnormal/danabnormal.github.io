Married father-of-two Bob Miller was checking for occupants in the three- storey building, in Morledge Street. Mr Miller, aged 44 and with 26 years' experience, is the first Leicestershire firefighter to die on duty since 1975. Fire union chiefs and employers began a second day of talks on their pay row with a minute's silence for Mr Miller.
